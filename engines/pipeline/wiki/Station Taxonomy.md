# Station Taxonomy

## Intake

Hot folders, cold sweeps, NAS drops, cloud drops, clipboard drops.

## Identity

Classification, series detection, domain/law tags, axiom and 7Q candidates.

## Transformation

Lossless text, HTML, Markdown, JSON, TTS, STT, video package, thumbnails, captions.

## Verification

Paper grader, rigor gate, contradiction scan, duplicate scan, evidence/citation check, Lean candidate gate.

## Publication

AI portal, vector DB, wiki/Obsidian export, R2 publish, final package.

## Reciprocal Signals

Gap detection, duplicate alerts, quality loops, missing-paper creation requests.
