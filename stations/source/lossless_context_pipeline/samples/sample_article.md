# Grace Entropy Bridge

This paper argues that grace limits moral entropy because restoration requires an external source of order.

## Definition

Moral entropy means the drift of a will-bearing system away from coherence.

## Equation

The proposed relation is $dS_m/dt = \sigma - W_{grace}/T$ where the grace term opposes disorder.

## Evidence

The claim is compared to thermodynamic entropy and information loss [1].

## Kill Condition

The model fails if a closed moral system restores coherence without external grace.

## Boundary

Entropy is used physically in thermodynamics and interpretively in theology; the bridge must be stated rather than assumed.
