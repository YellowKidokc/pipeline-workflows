# Pilot Pre-Flight Checklist

This document is a final operational checklist for pilots before takeoff.

The purpose is to ensure that the aircraft, crew, instruments, fuel, communication systems, and safety controls are ready before flight.

The checklist must be completed in order. If any required item fails, takeoff must stop until the issue is corrected.

## Checklist

1. Confirm aircraft identity and flight number.
2. Verify fuel level.
3. Inspect engine status.
4. Confirm navigation instruments.
5. Test radio communication.
6. Confirm weather clearance.
7. Verify runway assignment.
8. Confirm crew readiness.
9. Review emergency procedures.
10. Authorize takeoff only after all checks pass.

## Kill Condition

This checklist fails if pilots cannot determine whether a required safety item passed or failed.

## Risk

Failure to complete this checklist could lead to aircraft malfunction, communication failure, or unsafe takeoff.
