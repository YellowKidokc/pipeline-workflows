# Paper Rubric Output Prompt

Use station outputs to generate:

1. `{paper_id}_RUBRIC.xlsx`
2. `{paper_id}_REPORT.html`

The Excel workbook is the exhaustive audit truth. The HTML report is the
polished front side for human readers.

Follow the schema in:

`docs/rubric-output-schema.md`

Do not hide failed checks. Keep unresolved items visible.
